Welcome to The Imaginary Road

Welcome! My name is Michael Collins and this is my website. The Imaginary Road is my personal website and hosts my blog. I like to write about and share my thoughts and lessons learned from my life and my career as a professional software developer. Please feel free to look around, read my blog, and learn more about me.

I am a professional software developer with 26 years of professional experience. I currently work at Neudesic and work in Desert Mountain region which covers the Phoenix, Arizona metropolitan region; Denver, Colorado; and Las Vegas, Nevada. At Neudesic, I serve as a Technical Client Director in our Digital Platforms division where I focus on building custom software products, business applications, and mobile applications for customers and enterprises.

I do a lot of hobby development and projects on the side. I am working on a lot of great ideas and I'm hoping to soon start a side career as an independent software publisher. I will be writing a lot on my blog about the ideas that I am developing and my journey to releasing them in the marketplace.

Welcome to my website! Please come back frequently to read about my journey!
